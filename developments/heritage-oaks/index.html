Heritage Oaks

Heritage Oaks is a new development located in northwest Midland. New homes starting at $280,000 (minimum 1,600 square feet livable area) are available. Our model home is located at 5800 Llano.

Plans for Heritage Oaks include a swimming pool and cabana for residents. The development is within walking distance of C.J. Kelly Park, Nueva Vista Golf Club, and the Putt Putt Fun Center, and has convenient access to Loop 250 and a wide variety of shopping venues.

Heritage Oaks is served by the following Midland Independent School District campuses:

  • Scharbauer Elementary
  • Goddard Jr. High
  • Midland High School
